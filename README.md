# D-Mart-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive D-Mart Sales Analysis Dashboard developed in Power BI to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, order fulfillment, payment trends, shipping preferences, and demographic distribution. The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into actionable business insights that support data-driven decision-making.

The objective of this project is to identify key sales trends, evaluate operational performance, and uncover opportunities to improve customer satisfaction and business profitability.

🎯 Business Objectives
Analyze overall sales performance and order trends.
Monitor customer purchasing behavior across different regions.
Evaluate order fulfillment efficiency and cancellation rates.
Understand payment method preferences.
Assess the impact of discounts on sales.
Analyze shipping methods and customer satisfaction levels.
Provide actionable recommendations for business growth.

📈 Dashboard Features
1. Sales Trend Analysis
Monthly sales performance from 2021–2023.
Identification of seasonal peaks and low-performing periods.
Sales trend visualization using line charts.
2. Customer Demographics
State-wise customer distribution analysis.
Comparison of customer concentration across regions.
Interactive filtering by location.
3. Order Fulfillment Analysis
Delivered, Shipped, Cancelled, and Returned orders.
Funnel chart visualization for order lifecycle tracking.
Performance monitoring of fulfillment operations.
4. Discount vs Sales Analysis
Evaluation of discounted and non-discounted sales contribution.
Impact assessment of promotional pricing strategies.
5. Payment Method Analysis
Revenue distribution across:
Debit Card
Credit Card
UPI
Net Banking
Cash on Delivery (COD)
6. Shipping Performance
Analysis of customer shipping preferences.
Comparison of Free Shipping, Priority, and Express Plus services.
7. Customer Satisfaction Analysis
Average customer rating measurement.
Evaluation of customer experience and service quality.

🔍 Key Insights
Sales Performance
Generated $16.95M in total sales from 25K orders.
Monthly sales remained stable between $0.40M and $0.54M.
Consistent sales growth indicates strong customer demand and retention.

Customer Distribution
Customer base is evenly distributed across:
Maharashtra
Andhra Pradesh
Gujarat
Telangana

Order Fulfillment
Majority of orders were successfully delivered.
Cancellation rate stands at 7.14%, highlighting opportunities for operational improvement.

Payment Preferences
Debit and Credit Cards contribute the largest share of revenue.
UPI has emerged as a strong digital payment channel.

Shipping Trends
Free Shipping is the most preferred delivery option.
Customers show lower adoption of premium shipping services.

Customer Satisfaction
Average rating of 4.0/5 reflects a positive customer experience.
